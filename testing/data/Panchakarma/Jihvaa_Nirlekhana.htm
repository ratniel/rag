Chikitsa Prakaara: Jihvaa Nirlekhana

 

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:

        Cleaning the tongue.

        Cleaning of tongue with Lekhan`ee [Stick] in the direction of the tongue is termed as Jihvaa Nirlekhana.

 

Pradhaanakarma:

        The cleaning should be done with the help of a stick of 10 Angula diameter made up of gold, silver, copper or a tender stem.

        The stick should be Mridu (soft) and S`hlakshn`a.

        The sticks should be rubbed gently over the tongue.

        Precaution should be taken not to injure the tongue while rubbing.

 

Other Description:

        Indications and various Dravya(sticks) to be used can be got through Anveshak search facility.
