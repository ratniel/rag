·        Application of oil over head region is termed as Moordha Taila.

 

Pradhaanakarma:

·        There are four types of Moordha Taila

1.    S`hirobyanga,

2.    S`hirodhaaraa,

3.    S`hirobasti and

4.    S`hira Pichu

 

Chikitsaakarma:

·        Kesha Drid`hatva (strengthens hair)

·        S`hira Kapaalaasthi Balya (strengthens bones of head)

·        Indriya Prasannatva

·        Mukha Kaantivardhaka

·        Sukha Nidraa
