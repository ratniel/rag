Chikitsaa Prakaara: Anagni Sveda Bahupaana

 

Prakaara Paribhaashaa:

∑        This is a type of Anagni Svedana.

∑        Bahupaana means drinking alcohol in a large quantity.

 

Chikitsaa Karma:

∑        When alcohol is consumed in a large quantity i.e. more than advisable, the Drava Gun`a (liquidity) inside the body increases. Due to Vyavaayee and Vikaasee properties of Madya it expels Jaat`haraagnee out of the body and hence fomentation occurs.
