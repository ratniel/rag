Chikitasa Prakaara: Holaaka Sveda

 

Poorvakarma (Prerequisites):

        Instrument checklist: A heap of excreta of elephants, horses or camels of the size of the bed is to be ignited. When it is well burnt and has become smokeless a bed covered with a thin sheet is placed over it.

        Patient preparation: Body massage.

 

Pradhaanakarma:

        The patient well covered should lie down over this bed to receive the heat.

 

Pas`hchaatkarma:

        One should avoid direct contact with open air or cold water immediately after Svedana.

        Sweat should be wiped off completely.

 

Other Description:

        Precaution should be taken to avoid Dagdha.
