Granthi Chikitsaa

 

Reference:

C.Chi.12, S.Chi.18, A.H.U.30, N.R. Galagan`d`a, Gan`d`amaalaa, Apachee, Granthi, Arbuda

 

Introduction:

Granthi is a localized growth manifesting with a symptom S`hotha and which resembles a bunch or protuberance of any kind.

 

Saamaanya Chikitsaa:

1.    Snehana, Svedana

2.    Vamana, Virechana

3.    Granthi Svedana à Pakvaavasthaa

4.    Samoola Paat`ana

5.    Agnikarma

6.    Vran`akarma

 

There are chances of formation of Granthi again; if cauterization is done improperly (Asamyak Dagdha), or if a surgeon keeps remnants at the time of removal of Granthi. Threfore, for a surgeon it is necessary to remove Granthi with its capsule (Kos`ha). Afterwards, it is to be treated as Vran`a.

 

Vishesha Chikitsaa

 

Vaataja Granthi Chikitsaa (Treat as Vaataja S`hopha.)

1.    Upanaaha Sveda

2.    Lepa àPakva Avasthaa

3.    Vidaaran`a (Pooya Nis`hkaasana)

4.    Vran`a S`hodhana

5.    Vran`a Ropan`a

Pittaja Granthi Chikitsaa. (Treat as Pittaja S`hopha.)

1.    Raktamokshan`a by Jalaukaa

2.    S`heeta Chikitsaa (Aabhyantara - S`heeta Gun`aatmaka Kvaatha or Svarasa and Baahya - S`heeta Parisheka & Lepa.)

 

Kaphaja Granthi Chikitsaa. (Treat as Kaphaja S`hopha.)

1.    Snehana, Svedana

2.    Vamana, Virechana

3.    Raktamoks`han`a

4.    Vimlaapana

5.    Lepa

Raktaja Granthi Chikitsaa

1.    Raktamokshan`a by Jalaukaa

2.    S`heeta Chikitsaa (Aabhyantara: S`heeta Kvaatha / Svarasa, S`heeta Parisheka & Lepa.)

Maamsa Granthi Chikitsaa and Vran`a Granthi Chikitsaa

1.    Paatana

2.    Prakshaalaana (Kvaatha indicated in Kaphaja Vran`a)

3.    Vran`a S`hodhana

4.    Vran`a Ropan`a
