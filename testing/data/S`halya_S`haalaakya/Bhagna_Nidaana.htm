Bhagna

 

Reference:

Su.Ni.15, A.H.U.27, M.N.44

 

Types:

Sandhi Mukta: It is dislocation of joints.

Kaan`d`a Bhagna: It is fracture of bones.

 

Sandhi Mukta:

1.    Utpisht`a: Ubhayatah S`hopha, Vedanaa Praadurbhaava, are different types of pains at the night.

2.    Vis`hlisht`a: Mild s`hopha, Vedanaa Saatatya, SandhiVikriyaa

3.    Vivartita: Vishama Angataa, Vedanaa

4.    Avakshipta: Sandhi Vis`hlesha, Teevra Rujatva

5.    Atikshipta: Sandhi Asthnoh Atikraantataa and Vedanaa

6.    Tiryak Kshipta: Ekaasthi Paars`hvaapagamana, Ati Vedanaa

 

Kaan`d`a Bhagna:

1.    Karkat`aka: This Bhagna is elevated like Granthi

2.    As`hvakarn`a: This Bhagna is elevated like As`hvaKarn`a

3.    Choorn`ita: This Bhagna produces sound after touch.

4.    Pichchhita: This Bhagna is wide spread, there is little S`hopha

5.    Asthichchhallita: In this Bhagna Asthi gets cut at the lateral side

6.    Kaan`d`abhagna: In this Bhagna there is Kampa

7.    Majjaanugata: In this Bhagna Majjaa has came out.

8.    Atipaatita: In this Bhagna Asthi is completely cut down.

9.    Vakra: This Bhagna is Aabhugna i.e. little curved.

10.           Chhinna: This Bhagna is Anya Tara Paars`hvaavashisht`am

11.           Paat`ita: In this Bhagna there is pain. This Bhagna is Bahu Vidaarita

12.           Sphut`ita: It is like S`hookaPoorn`a iva aadhmaatam and Sphut`ita

 

General Symptoms and Signs of Sandhi Mukta Bhagna:

        Prasaaran`a As`hakti

        Aakunchana As`hakti

        Vivartana As`hakti

        Aakshepan`a As`hakti

        Ugra Rujaa

        Spars`haasahatva

 

General Symptoms and Signs of Kaan`d`a Bhagna:

        S`hvayathu Baahulya

        Spandana

        Vivartana

        Spars`haasahatva

        Avapeed`yamaane S`habdah

        Srastaangataa

        Vividha Vedanaa Praadurbhaava

        Sarvaavasthaasu Na S`harmalaabha
