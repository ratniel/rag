∑        The process by which the wound edges are softened is termed as Mridvikaran`a.

 

Pradhaanakarma:

∑        During process of healing the wound edges become hard and rough and thus healing is hampered. This procedure helps in softening the wound edges.

∑        This should be achieved by Raktamokshan`a, Snehana, Svedana and Gaad`ha Bandha.

∑        This can be achieved by sprinkling of Madhura and Snigdha drugs.

∑        Various formulations used can be got through Anveshak search facility.
