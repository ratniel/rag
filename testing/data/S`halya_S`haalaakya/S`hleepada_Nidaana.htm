S`hleepada

 

Reference:

M. Ni.39, C. Chi.12, S. Ni.12, A. H. U. 29

Nirukti

 

S`hilaavat Padam S`hleepadam |

M. Ni. 39/1-3

 

Introduction and Sampraapti:

 

  • S`hleepada manifests on the Janghaa, S`hisna, Osht`ha, Naasaa.

 

  • It also manifests on Greevaa, Vamkshan`a, Janghaa, Osht`ha, Paada, Karn`a according to Pushkalaavata.

 

  • It may appear on hands also. S. Ni. 12/15

 

  • There is vitiation of Maamsa and Medo Dhaatu. C. Chi. 12/98; Chakrapaan`i

 

  • S`hleepada is observed in the territories where ponds are found more (Aanoopa Des`ha) and where the climate is cold.

 

  • S`hleepada is caused because of the vitiation of the Kapha, Maamsa and Rakta. It causes swelling in the calf region (Janghaa), lower part of the leg (Pin`d`i), front portion of the leg (Prapada).

 

  • Aggravated Dosha, traverse to the downward direction towards Vamkshan`a, Ooru, Jaanu, Janghaa. Afterwards, they reside in the foot manifesting S`hotha gradually.
  • This is S`hleepada.

 

  • All the types of S`hleepada are necessarily caused by Kapha Prakopa; as Gurutva and Mahatva is not possible without Kapha.

 

Swaroopa

 

Rogamaarga Baahya

Avayava Janghaa, Med`hra, Osht`ha, Naasaa, Greevaa, Vamkshan`a, Paada, Karn`a, Hasta

Dosha Kapha Prakopa, Vaata Prakopa, Pitta Prakopa

Dhaatu Maamsa Dusht`i, Medo Dusht`I, Rakta Dusht`i

Srotas Maamsavaha Srotas, Medovaha Srotas, Raktavaha Srotas

 

Types

1.   Vaataja

2.   Pittaja

3.   Kaphaja

 

Vaataja S`hleepada

It is Khara, Krishn`a in color Parusha, Vaata, Vedanaa, Parisphot`ana.

 

Pittaja S`hleepada

It is Peetaavabhaasee, Mridu. It manifests with Jvara, Daaha.

 

Kaphaja S`hleepada

It is S`hveta, Snigdhaavabhaasee and having relatively mild pain. There is heaviness. It resembles to Mahaaganthi. It is associated with thorns (Kan`t`akai Upachitam).
