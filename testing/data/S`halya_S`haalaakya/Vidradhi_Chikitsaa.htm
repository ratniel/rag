Vidradhi Chikitsaa

 

Reference:

A.H.Chi. 13, S.Chi. 16, C.Su.17, N.R. Vidradhi

 

Saamanya Chikitsaa:

  • Vidradhi in primordial stage should be treated as Apakva Vran`a S`hotha and Rakta Mokshan`a should be done frequently.
  • Vidradhi in stage of suppuration should be treated as Vran`a (Bhinna)
  • In all types of Vidradhi in Pakva /Apakva stage Vidradhi Naas`haka Kashaaya (decoction) should be given combined with S`hilaajitu or Guggulu.
  • Vidradhi should be treated in Apakvavasthaa (initial stage) and Pakvavasthaa/Paaka should be avoided as it becomes suppurative faster than Vran`a hence, it is termed as Vidradhi.

 

Treatment of Vaataja Vidradhi: - (Aamavasthaa)

 

  • Sechana with Brihatpanchamoola Kvaatha
  • Lepa of Vaatghna Gan`a Sneha
  • Upanaaha(Poultice) of Audaka / Anoopa Maamsa, Amla Lavan`a Siddha Sneha,Ves`havaara, Kris`haraa.
  • Rakatamoks`han`a
  • Siraavedha should be done at Koorpara Sandhi (at Elbow Joint).
  • Svedana with Dugdha and Paayasa.
  • Vran`a Ropan`a with Bhadradaarvaadi Gan`a
  • S`hodhana with Trivritadi Gan`a Sneha
  • Vran`a Ropan`a with Vidaryaadi Trivritta Gan`a Sneha (After Vidradhi Bhedana)

 

Vaataja Vidradhi: - (Pakvavasthaa)

 

  • Bhedana followed by Vran`a S`hodhana and Sechana with Brihatpanchamoola Kvaatha
  • Vran`a Ropan`a with Bhadradaarvaadi Gan`a, Saalavan`a Taila.

 

Treatment of Pittaja Vidradhi: -

 

  • Sechana with Ksheeri Vriksha Siddha Jala / Kvaatha
  • Lepa with Yasht`yaadi Kalka, Saarivaadi Kalka
  • Vran`a Ropan`a with Nyagrodhaadi Gan`a Siddha Ghrita and Manjist`haadi Kalka(After Vidradhi Bhedana)
  • Seka with Jeevaneeya Ghrita with S`harkaraa, Ikshu Rasa, Dugdha
  • Raktamokshan`a
  • Siraavedha should be done at Koorpara Sandhi (at Elbow Joint).
  • To pacify Daaha and Vedanaa (pain and burning sensation) purgation with Trivritaadi Choorn`a should be administered
  • Parisheka with Ksheeri Vriksha Kashaaya and Audaka Dravya Kashaaya

 

 

Treatment of Kaphaja Vidradhi: -

 

  • Rooksha Svedana with Isht`ikaa, Sikataa, Losht`a, S`hakrit, Tusha, Paamshu and Gomootra.
  • Kashaayapaana
  • Vamana
  • Aalepa
  • Raktamokshan`a with (Alaabu) (Refer: - Alaabu Karma)
  • Siraavedha in mid axillary line (Vaama Paarshva Kakshaa Stanantarashraya Adhah Des`ha) between Axilla and Mammary gland.
  • Sechana / Dhaavana with Aaragvadhaadi Gan`a Siddha Jala
  • Lepa with Saktvaadi lepa
  • Vran`a Ropan`a with Kulaatthaadi Taila (After Vidradhi Bhedana)

 

Treatment of Raktaja Vidradhi: -

 

  • Same as Pittaja Vidradhi
  • Siraavedhaa should be done at Koorpara Sandhi (at Elbow Joint).

 

Treatment of Aagantuja Vidradhi:

 

  • Same as Pittaja Vidradhi

 

Treatment of Kshataja Vidradhi: -

 

  • Same as Pittaja Vidradhi

 

Treatment of Aabhyantara / Antarvidradhi: -

  • General treatment: -

o       Snehana

o       Svedana

o       Gulma Chikitsaa

 

 

 

  • Aamaavasthaa:

 

o       Vaarun`aadi Gan`a Kvaatha with Ooshakaadi Gan`a Kalka Prakshepa

o       Ghrita made of Vaarun`aadi Gan`a, Ooshakaadi Gan`a and Vairechanika Dravya_s in morning.

o       Vaarun`aadi Gan`a, Ooshakaadi Gan`a Vairechanika Dravya_s Siddha Taila should be used for Aasthaapana /Anuvaasana Basti.

o       Madhu S`higru Kalka + Yathaa Dosha Kalka Avaapa (to pacify the specific Dosha_s)

o       Yathaa Dosha Kalka Kvaatha added with S`hilaajatu (to pacify the specific Dosha_s)

o       Guugulu Pradhaana Devadaaru Kvaatha (for Vaatapraadhaanya) or S`hunt`hee Siddha Kvaatha (for Kaphapraadhaanya)

o       Snehana

o       Upanaaha

o       Anulomana

o       Siraavedha should be in mid axillary line (Vaama Paarshva Kakshaa Stanantara) if Vidradhi is in left side. (S.Sha 8)

o       Siraavedha should be in mid axillary line (Dakshin`a Parshva Kakshaa Stanantara) if Vidradhi is in right side. (S.Sha 8)

 

Treatment for Aabhyaantra Vidradhi with Raktadusht`i and Gambheeranugatatva: -(Deep situated internal abscess with Vitiated blood)

Siraavedha should be done in mid axillary line (Kakshaa Stanaantara Adhah Desha) between Axilla and Mammary gland in lower side (S. Sha. 8)

 

Vidradhi Chikitsaa:

In Vidradhi bloodletting is done with the help of S`hringa etc. adjacent to the region of abscess (Vidradhi) from Siraa.

 

Treatment of Antarvidradhi:

In internal abscess treatment should be same as Gulma considering Dosha_s.

 

  • Pachyamaanaavasthaa:

 

o       Vidradhi situated in Kost`ha when attains Pachyamaanavasthaa protrudes externally, indicating this protuberance as the site of affliction.

o       Upanaaha should be applied locally to attain Pakva / Bhinna Avasthaa.

 

Examination of Pakva / Bhinna or Pakvaavasthaa:

1.    Regression of tenderness locally.

2.    Regression of symptoms like burning sensation.

3.    Oozing of the vitiated Dosha_s from the Vran`a.

 

  • Treatment of Vidradhi Pakvaavasthaa:

 

o       Similar to Vran`a Chikitsaa

o       Allow Pus discharge through (Oordhva /Adhah) upper / lower parts of body to flow off.

 

 
  Vaarun`aadi Gan`a Kvaatha Madhu S`higru Kalka/Yavaagoo.

 

o       Management of complications.

o       After 10- 12 days S`hodhana with Trayamaan`a / Tailvaka Ghrita

o       Tiktaka Ghrita Paana.

 

Treatment in Raktaja Pittaja VaatajaVidradhi: -

Siraavedha should be done at Baahu (at Elbow Joint).

 

  • Treatment of Pakva Vidradhi: -

o       Bhedana

o       Vran`a vat Upachaara

 

  • Treatment of Pakva Vidradhi Bhinna / Sraavee Avasthaa: -

 

o       Vaarun`aadi Gana or Madhu S`higru Choorn`a added in Maireeya, Dhaanyaamla, Suraa, Aasava

o       S`higru Moola Jaala Siddha Sidhharthaka Odana

o       Yava Kola Kulattha Yoosha.

o       Tilvaka Ghrita (of Vaatvyadhi) in a low dose.

o       Trivritaadi Gan`a Kvaatha

 

 

Treatment of Asthimajja Gata Vidradhi (Osteomyelitis)

  • Snehana
  • Svedana
  • Raktamokshan`a
  • Asthi Bhedana

 

Treatment of Vidradhi Vran`a -Devoid of Pus and foreign body

 

  • Vran`a S`hodhana
  • Vran`a Dhaavana with Tikta Dravya Kvaatha.
  • Tikta Dravya Siddha Ghrita Paana.

 

Treatment of Majjagata Vidradhi (Sraavi Avasthaa)

Sams`hodhana Dravya_s Kvaatha

 

Treatment of Stana Vidradhi

 

  • Similar to Vran`a S`hotha Chikitsaa
  • Similar to Vidradhi Chikitsaa

 

Special precaution: Avoid Upanaaha.

  • Paat`ana S`hastrakarma is done avoiding Stanyavaha Siraa and Krishn`a Chuchuka (areola)
  • Stana Nirdohana(milk extraction) in all type of stages (Aama Pachyamaana Pakva Avasthaa )

 

Pathya:

 

Apakvaavasthaa

  • Dhaanya- Jeern`a S`haalee, Kulattha
  • S`haaka- Las`hoona, Rakta S`higru, Nishpaava, Kaaravellaka, Punarnavaa, Naravela,Chitraka, S`hreeparn`ee
  • Other-Madhu

 

Pakvaavasthaa

  • Dhaanya- Jeern`a S`haalee, Raktas`haalee
  • S`haaka- S`haalincha S`haaka, Pat`ola, Karpoora, Chandana
  • S`himbee- Mudga
  • Dugdha- Ghrita
  • Drava- Taila, S`hritas`heetajala
  • Phala- Kadalee
  • Other- Man`d`a, Peyaa
