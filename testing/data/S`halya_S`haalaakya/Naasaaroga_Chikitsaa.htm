Naasaa Roga

 

Reference:

C.Chi.26, A.H.U.20, S.U.23, 24, N.R. Naasaa Roga

 

Introduction

  • Naasaa Roga is diseases manifested in the nose. Especially, these diseases are related to the Naasaa Srotas.
  • These are Madhyama Maargagata Vyaadhi.
