Mada

Reference:

A.H.Chi.7.

 

Introduction:

Mada literally means excitement or hilarity.

 

Saamaanya Chikitsaa:

        As Pitta and Vaata Dosha is in dominance in Mada, general treatment includes Pittaghna, Vaataghna, S`hodhana and S`hamana Chikitsaa along with Satvaavajaya and Daivavyapaas`hraya Chikitsaa.

        S`hodhana Chikitsaa includes Nasya, Basti, Raktamokshan`a

        S`hamana Chikitsaa includes Formulations like Shat`pala Ghrita, Dhaatryaadi Ghrita, Kalyaan`aka Ghrita etc.

        Chiktsaa similar to Moorchchhaa should also be adopted

        Daivavyapaas`hraya Chikitsaa Man`i

        Satvaavajaya Chikitsaa Harshana Chikitsaa, Aas`hvaasana, Sadaachaara
