Udaavarta Chikitsaa

 

Reference:

C. Su. 7, S. U. 55, A. H. Su.4, N.R. Udaavarta

 

Introduction:

When natural urges are suppressed or obstructed, they lead to an upward or retrograde movement of the responsible Vaata, this is termed as Udaavarta.
