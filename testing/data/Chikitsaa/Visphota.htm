Visphot`a Chikitsaa

 

Reference:

Y.R.(Uttaraardha), B.R., N.R. Visphot`a

 

Introduction:

The blisters which resemble to the blisters due to burn and manifests all over the body are caused due to Rakta and Pitta. They are called as Visphot`a.

 

Saamaanya Chikitsaa:

       S`hodhana: Vamana, Virechana

       Lepa: Nimba, Chandana, Taila, Karpoora, Tila

       Langhana

       Aahaara: Puraan`a Dhaanya, Tikta S`haaka

       Rakta Dusht`ihara and Pittaghna: Aahaara, Vihaara and Aushadha Sevana

       Anarha Chikitsaa Karma: Svedana
