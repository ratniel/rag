Vaatavyaadhi Chikitsaa

Reference:

Ch.Chi.28, N.R. Vaatavyaadhi

 

Chikitsaa:

Before treating any Vaatavyaadhi Physician should think of following things:

 

  • Sthaana: Pakvaas`haya, Aamaas`haya etc. Physician should give first preference to treatment of Balavaana Dosha. In case of Anubandha of Pitta & Kapha, Physicain should treat Pitta first because it is more Aas`hukaaree than Kapha.

 

  • Dooshya: Rasa, Rakta etc. Dhaatu

 

  • Aavaran`a

 

Physician should consider following Chikitsaa Avasthaa:

  • Nirupastambhita: a) Saamaanya b) Vis`hesha Chikitsaa
  • Upastambhita: a) Saamaanya b) Vis`hesha Chikitsaa

 

Nirupastambhita:

 

  • Saamaanya Chikitsaa Krama:

 

o             Snehapaana

o             Snehaklaanta

o             As`hvaasana (Vis`hraama)

o             Dugdhapaana (Punah Snehanaartha)

o             Aahaara: - Yoosha, Aanoopa Maamsarasa, Graamya Maamsarasa + Sneha, Paayasa, Kris`haraa (Amla & Lavan`a Rasayukta)

o             Panchakarma: - Anuvaasana Basti & Nasya

o             Tarpaka Aahaara

o             Snehana

o             Svedana ( Snehasamyukta Naad`ee / Prastara / Sankara / Kut`ee etc. Sveda )

o             Sams`hodhana

o             Samsarjana Karma

o             Agni Deepana

o             Snehana followed by Svedana

o             Constant use of Amla, Lavan`a & Snehayukta Aahaara

o             Nasya & Dhoomapaana

 

  • Aahaara: - Ghrita, Taila, Vasaa, Majjaa, Maamsarasa, Madhura, Amla & Lavan`a Rasayukta Aahaara, Brihan`a Aahaara.

 

  • Chikitsaa Karma: - Parisheka, Abhyanga, Basti, Snehana, Svedana.

 

  • Vihaara: - Praavaran`a, Nivaata S`hayana.

 

  • Kalpa: - Vaatahara Dravya Siddha S`hatapaakee / Sahastrapaakee Taila, Taila Kalpana used in Vaatarakta useful in Vaatavyaadhi.

 

Aavasthika Chikitsaa: -

o             Avasthaa: - Stambha / Harsha / Toda / Aayaama / S`hotha / Gaurava - Abhyanga (Ekaanga / Sarvaanga) & Svedana.

o             Effect of all above treatment: - Kosht`ha Maardavata: - Which in turn pacify Vaatavyaadhi.

o             Avasthaa: - Bahudhaavasthaa: - Snehayukta Virechana: - Tilvaka Siddha Yoga / Saatalaa Siddha Ghrita / Erand`a Taila Yoga.

o             Hetu Pratyanika Chikitsaa: - Snigdha, Amla, Lavan`a & Ushn`a Aahaara leads to Mala Sanchiti in Pureeshavaha Srotasa: - Vaata Pratiloma: - Vaataanulomaka Chikitsaa.

o             Avasthaa: - Vaatavyaadhi + Durbala Rugn`a + Virechana Anarha: - Nirooha Basti followed by Paachaka & Deepaka Aahaara.

 

  • Vis`hesha Chikitsaa:

 

o             Kosht`hagata Vaata: - Mala Paachanaartha: - Kshaara, Paachana, Deepana & Amla Padaartha. (Search for Amla Rasa Dravya + Ka

o             Gudagata Vaata: - Similar Chikitsaa: - Udaavarta. (See Udaavarta Chikitsaa)

o             Pakvaas`hayagata Vaata: - Similar Chikitsaa: - Udaavarta.

o             Sarvaangagata Vaata: - Nirooha & Anuvaasana Basti.

o             Tvakgata Vaata: - Abhyanga + Avagaaha Sveda + Hitakara Annapaana + Hridya Annapaana.

o             Raktagata Vaata: - Virechana + Raktamokshan`a.

o             Maamsagata Vaata: - Virechana + Nirooha Basti + Vaataghna Chikitsaa.

o             Medogata Vaata: - Virechana + Nirooha Basti + Vaataghna Chikitsaa.

o             Asthimajjaagta Vaata: - Baahya Snehana & Aabhyantara Snehapaana.

o             S`hukragata Vaata: - Harshan`a + Balavardhaka Aahaara + S`hukravardhaka Aahaara.

o             S`hukraavarodha: - Virechana: - Peyaa, Vilepee etc. Sansarjana Krama: - Jaat`haraagni Deepana: - S`hukravardhaka Chikitsaa.

o             Garbhaas`hayagata Vaata: - S`hushka Garbha :- Yasht`yaadi Siddha Ksheera.

o             Hridayagata Vaata: - S`haaleeparn`ee Siddha Ksheera.

o             Naabhigata Vaata: - Bilva Kvaatha Siddha Matsya Maamsa.

o             Vesht`na: - Upanaaha Sveda.

o             Samkocha: - Maasha Saidhava Taila Abhyanga.

o             Baahugata Vaata: - Nasya + Auttara Bhaktika Sneha.

o             S`hirogata Vaata: - Nasya + Auttara Bhaktika Sneha.

o             Naabhi Adhobhaagagata Vaata: - Basti + Ghrita Paana followed by Annapaana.

o             Ardit: - Nasya + S`hirah Tarpan`a (S`hirobasti, S`hirodhaaraa, S`hiropichu)+ Naad`ee Sveda + Aanoopa Maamsa Upanaaha.

o             Pakshaaghaata: - Svedana + Snehayukta Virechana.

o             Gridhrasee: - Agnikarma (Near Kan`d`araa + Gulpha).

o             Khallee :- Snehayukta Paayasa / Kris`haraa / Maamsa.

o             Vivrita Mukha: - Svedana + Joint fixing.

 

Upastambhita: -

 

  • Saamaanya Chikitsaa: -

 

o             Chikitsaa According to type of Vaata: -

         Udaana Vaayu: - Vamana.

         Apaana Vaayu: - Anulomana.

         Samaana Vaayu: - S`hamana.

         Vyaana Vaayu: - Vamana, Anulomana, S`hamana.

         Praan`a Vaayu: - Physician should give first priority to treatment of Praan`a Vaayu.

 

o             Kalpa /Aahaara / Dravya: - Should be of following characteristics:

Anabhishyandi, Snigdha, Srotovis`hodhaka, Vaataanulomaka, Kapha Pitta Avirodhee, Rasaayana (e.g. S`hilaajatu, Guggula, Dugdha, Chyavanapraash`a, Braahma Rasaayana etc.) are useful in Vaatavyaadhi.

 

o             Chikitsaa Karma: - Abhyanga, Basti, Yaapana Basti, Madhura Rasa Dravya Siddha Anuvaasana Basti.

 

o             Pittavrit Vaata: -

         Saamaanya Chikitsaa Sootra: - Pittaghna + Vaata Avirodhi Annapaana, Aushadha

         S`heeta & Ushn`a Vyatyaasaat Chikitsaa, Jeevaneeya Ghrita

         Karma: - Yaapana Basti, Kseera Basti, Virechana, Parisheka

 

o             Kaphaavrit Vaata: -

         Kaphaghna + Vaata Avirodhi Annapaana, Aushadha

         Aahaara: - Yava Bhojya, Jaangal Maamsa, Puraan`a Ghrita, Tila Taila, Sarshapa Taila

         Karma: - Teekshn`a Svedana, Nirooha Basti, Vamana, Virechana

 

  • Vishesha Chikitsaa: -

 

o             Aamaas`hayagata Kapha: - Vamana

o             Pakvaa`hayagata Kapha: - Virechana

o             Sarva S`hareeragata Pitta: - Virechana

o             Avasthaa: - Svedana: - Pakvaas`hayagata Kapha or Pitta: - Basti

o             Avasthaa: - Vaata Kaphaanubandhi: - Gomutra Yukta Nirooha Basti

o             Avasthaa: - Vaata Pittaanubandhi: - Nirooha Basti(Dugdha Yukta ) or Anuvaasana Basti ( Madhura Skandha Siddha Taila )

o             S`hirogata Vaata + Kaphaanubandhi: - Dhoomapaana + Nasya

o             Raktaavrita Vaata: - Similar Chikitsaa Vaatarakta

o             Aamadoshaavrita Vaata: - Pramehaghna + Vaataghna + Medoghna

o             Maamsaavrita Vaata: - Sveda + Abhyanga + Maamsarasa + Dugdha + Ghrita

o             AshthiMajjaagata Vaata: - Mahaa Sneha

o             Annaavrita Vaata: - Vamana + Paachaka + Deepana + Laghu Aahaara

o             Mootraavrita Vaata: - Mootra Virechaka Aushadha + Svedana + Uttara Basti

o             Pureeshaavrit Vaata: - Eran`d`a Taila: - Snigdha Dravya Sevana + Anulomaka Chikitsaa (similar Chikitsaa: - Udaavarta)
